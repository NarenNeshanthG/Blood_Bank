Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a critical service supporting surgeries, cancer treatments, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries. A single donation can help save up to three lives.

Basic Eligibility Guidelines

While our AI provides a specific prediction, general requirements include:

  • Age: Typically 18-65 years old.
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Health: Good general health, feeling well on the day of donation.
  • Hemoglobin: Minimum levels apply (checked before donation).
  • Donation Interval: Usually 56 days (8 weeks) since last whole blood donation.
  • Risk Factors: No recent high-risk behaviors, travel to certain areas, specific medical conditions, or certain medications.

Note: Our registration form asks detailed questions used by the prediction model. Always be honest about your health history.
